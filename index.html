Welcome to The Eravati.

Nestled just moments from the iconic golden spire of Sule Pagoda, step through the carved gates of The Eravati and into a story written in stone and sunlight. Once the city's distinguised law courts, this heritage landmark - with its soraing colonnades and hallways that echo with history - has been lovingly transformed into a five-star sanctuary that honours Yangon's colonial-era grandeour while offering every modern luxury.
